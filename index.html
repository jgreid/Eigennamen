How to Play on Zoom:

  • Setup: Share this page's link with all players. Everyone opens the same link.
  • Spymasters: One person from each team enables "Spymaster View" to see the key (don't share screen!)
  • Host: One player shares their screen and clicks cards as teams make guesses.
  • Gameplay: Spymasters give one-word clues + a number. Their team guesses that many words.
  • Winning: Find all your team's words first! Avoid the assassin!
