How to Play:

  • Join a Team: Click "Join" under Red or Blue, or click "Spectate" to watch without a team.
  • Pick a Role: Each team needs one Spymaster (sees the key, gives clues) and one Clicker (clicks cards to reveal guesses).
  • Gameplay: Spymaster gives a clue, team discusses, Clicker clicks cards and ends the turn.
  • Tip: Your current role is shown in the banner above. Click a different button to change roles.
