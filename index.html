Game Rules

Two teams compete to find all their words first. Each team has a Spymaster who gives clues, and Clickers who reveal cards.

Roles

  • Spymaster: Sees the key. Gives one-word clues with a number.
  • Clicker: Reveals cards based on clues. Can end the turn.

Card Types

  • ■ Red: Red team's words
  • ● Blue: Blue team's words
  • ─ Neutral: Ends your turn
  • ✕ Assassin: Instant loss!

How to Play

  1. Teams take turns. The starting team has 9 words to find, the other has 8.
  2. The Spymaster gives a one-word clue and a number (how many cards relate).
  3. Clickers discuss and reveal cards one at a time.
  4. Revealing your color: keep going. Neutral: turn ends. Other team: turn ends. Assassin: you lose!
  5. First team to find all their words wins!

About Codenames Online

What is this?

A free, open-source implementation of the popular word game Codenames, designed for remote play over video calls or in person.

Features

  • Works offline - game state is encoded in the URL
  • Multiplayer mode with real-time sync
  • Custom word lists
  • Custom team names
  • Mobile-friendly design

How to Share

Share the game link or scan the QR code in the sidebar. All players see the same board when using the same link!

Codenames is a game by Vlaada Chvátil, published by Czech Games Edition.

This implementation is open source under the GPL v3.0 license.
