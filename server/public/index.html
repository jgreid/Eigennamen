      Help

      Two teams race to find all their words. Spymasters give clues, Clickers reveal cards.

      Card Types

      ■ Red Team ● Blue Team ◆ Neutral ☠ Assassin

      Turn Flow

      1. Spymaster gives a one-word clue + number
      2. Clickers reveal cards matching the clue
      3. Your color = keep going, Neutral/Enemy = turn ends
      4. Assassin = instant loss!

      Starting team finds 9 words, other team finds 8. First to find all wins!

      Eigennamen Mode

      A multi-round match where every card has a hidden point value. Teams play rounds until one reaches 42 points.

      Card Scores

      ★ Gold (+3) ◆ Silver (+2) ● Standard (+1) ○ Blank (0) ✕ Trap (-1)

      Match Flow

      1. Each round plays like a Vintage game
      2. When you reveal a card, your team earns its point value
      3. Round winner gets a +7 bonus
      4. First to 42 points (win by 3) wins!

      Only spymasters can see card scores before they are revealed. The assassin still ends the round instantly!
