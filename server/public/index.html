How to Play on Zoom:

  • Host: Creates game, shares screen, clicks cards. Click "Become Host" to take this role.
  • Spymasters: One per team clicks their team's Spymaster button. They see the key but CANNOT click cards.
  • Guessers: Watch the host's screen and call out guesses verbally.
  • Rotation: After each game, pick new spymasters! (The game will remind you.)
